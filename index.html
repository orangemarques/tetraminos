ABOUT


Tetraminos is a clone of tetris game created by Alexey Pajitnov, Dmitry Pavlovsky e Vadim Gerasimov in 1984.

In this version you can use touch feature of your WindowsPhone to move the tetraminos.



Enjoy yourself!
